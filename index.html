The Problem We’re Solving

Today, mobility is everything. We travel, commute, explore… but our devices still depend on power outlets that aren’t always available. And that’s where the problem starts — low battery means lost productivity, safety concerns, and daily inconvenience.

Why This Problem Matters

  • Dead phones in unfamiliar or unsafe places
  • Lost productivity during commutes and travel
  • Dependence on public charging points
  • Constant battery anxiety for heavy device users

SolVolt Solves This.

SolVolt is a smart, lightweight bag integrated with a high-efficiency solar panel that charges your phone, power bank, smartwatch, or any USB device — anytime, anywhere, using clean solar energy.

No extra weight. No separate panels. Just a bag you already carry, upgraded with sustainable, on-the-move power.

Who is SolVolt For?

Our target users? Students, travellers, trekkers, commuters — essentially anyone who carries a bag and carries a device.

Market Positioning

The market for portable clean-energy accessories is growing rapidly, and SolVolt positions itself at the intersection of lifestyle + sustainability + convenience.

Designing SolVolt

SolVolt is designed to be the bag you already carry — thoughtfully upgraded. No extra weight. No separate panels. Just seamless integration of solar power into everyday mobility.

Product Design Snapshot

The bag features a high-efficiency solar panel on its outer surface, discreet yet powerful. Inside, smart cable routing and a protected electronics compartment enable charging for your phone, power bank, smartwatch, or any USB device.

  • Integrated solar panel with reinforced outer layer
  • Internal USB hub / power routing
  • Ergonomic straps & lightweight materials
  • Weather-resistant exterior finish

SolVolt – planning, roadmap & strategy.

Roadmap & Strategy

Our near-term focus is on validating the core product experience, optimizing charging efficiency in real-world conditions, and ensuring durability for daily use.

  • Phase 1 – Prototype Refinement

    Iterating on bag design, solar efficiency, and user comfort based on pilot feedback.

  • Phase 2 – Strategic Partnerships

    Collaborating with manufacturing partners, material suppliers, and distribution channels.

  • Phase 3 – Launch & Positioning

    Targeted launch to students, travellers, trekkers, and commuters; building brand around lifestyle + sustainability + convenience.

Investment & Future Vision

With the right investment, we aim to scale production, enhance durability, and introduce smart features like battery storage and IoT-based energy tracking.

Planned Smart Features

  • Integrated battery storage for night-time charging
  • IoT-based energy tracking via mobile app
  • Usage analytics for performance optimization
  • Adaptive power routing to connected devices

Manufacturing & Scaling SolVolt

We are exploring partnerships for manufacturing and distribution so we can bring SolVolt from prototype to reality, at scale.

From Prototype to Production

Our manufacturing approach focuses on durability, material efficiency, and scalability. We aim to work with partners experienced in outdoor gear and electronics integration.

  • Selection of rugged fabrics and weather-resistant components
  • Quality control for solar panel performance & safety
  • Modular design for repairs and upgrades
  • Environmentally responsible production practices
